# Google Antigravity – Complete A–Z Website Generation Prompt

## Project Context

You are building a **production-ready, enterprise-grade SaaS web application** using **Next.js (latest stable version)**.

* **Product Name:** Ad2Go
* **Domain:** [https://www.ad2go.app](https://www.ad2go.app)
* **Type:** AI-powered Website & Google AdSense Readiness Analysis Platform

### Why This Product Exists

Ad2Go helps website owners, bloggers, and agencies understand **why their website gets rejected by Google AdSense** and **what exactly must be fixed before applying**.

Instead of guesswork and basic checklists, Ad2Go acts as a **decision-making system**, simulating Google-level logic by combining **technical, content, trust, SEO, and policy signals** into one intelligent report.

## What the System Does (Functional Context)

Users visit **[https://www.ad2go.app](https://www.ad2go.app)**, paste their website URL, and the system analyzes the website to determine **Google AdSense readiness**.

### Auto-Detected Features

* sitemap.xml (existence, validity, coverage)
* robots.txt (blocking issues, mistakes)
* Indexing signals (noindex, canonical)
* HTTPS & security signals
* Mobile friendliness
* Page structure & navigation

### Schema & Structured Data

* Detect existing schema markup
* Identify missing schema types
* Detect invalid or broken schema
* Suggest correct schema markup

### Website Dictionary (Advanced)

* Identify main site topics
* Detect risky or policy-sensitive vocabulary
* Detect thin or spam-like wording
* Detect AI-generated content signals
* Measure topic consistency across pages

### Content Intelligence

* Word count per page
* Content depth score
* Human vs AI probability
* Duplicate intent detection
* Copyright risk signals

### Trust & E-E-A-T Signals

* About page
* Contact page
* Privacy policy
* Disclaimer
* Author information
* Footer trust elements
* Brand consistency

### SEO & Performance

* Core Web Vitals
* Page speed
* Internal linking structure
* Orphan pages
* Broken links
* Title & meta structure
* Heading hierarchy (H1–H6)

### AdSense Policy Risk Engine

* Adult / gambling signals
* Misleading content
* Low-value pages
* Scraped or auto-generated content signals
* Risky layout or ad placement signals

---

## Decision Engine (Core Brain)

The system must:

* Combine all signals using weighted logic
* Calculate section-wise risk
* Produce a final verdict:

  * ❌ Not Ready
  * ⚠️ Fix & Apply
  * ✅ Ready for AdSense
